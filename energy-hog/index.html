Energy Hog

App, UI Design, Motion Graphics

Energy Hog is an electricity consumption monitor and advisor for personal spaces. Used for lowering your electricity bill and becoming as energy efficient as you can be.

Concept Objectives

The objective of this project was to create an app concept that implemented data visualizations. Everything beyond that was up to me. While charging my phone I wanted to visualize the wattage of all the devices I used in a day. Needless to say, I use a lot of technology and therefore, a lot of power.

I wanted to create a solution that helped me find which devices and appliances were hogging up all of my electricity. I wanted to find those devices and optimize their economical footprint.

A New Type of Outlet

I thought that a rough way to record wattage from most devices could work if the product used smarter outlets and power strips. The outlets could be made custom to read wattage and send that information to the internet. From the internet it could be seen on your phone or possibly other devices.
