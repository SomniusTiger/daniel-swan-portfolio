Greatest Hotels

Web Design, Branding

Greatest Hotels is a high class presentation of hotel resorts with Google business photos to drive traffic towards the greatest hotels in the world.

Concept Objectives

The objective of the project was designing a visual identity and website for the client, Greatest Hotels Group (GHG). The goal of the website was to curate the best hotels in the world and encourage users to explore them with Google Business Photos and promote direct reservations.

Users can learn all about the hotels they like, add them to lists, and become informed about their rooms and features. This project included user testing, user feedback, and personas to create a reliable tool for business or pleasure.

User Research

GHG is a collection of the greatest hotels in the world. By walking through luxury 4 and 5 star resorts, Greatest Hotels is focused on showcasing quality over value. Greatest Hotels’ target audience are upscale consumers looking to travel at 4 and 5 star resorts around the world. User Personas and design decisions were made with middle-aged americans in mind.
